About "PUGC SmartBot"

PUGC SmartBot is an AI-powered virtual assistant designed to simplify campus life for students, staff, and visitors of Punjab University Gujranwala Campus. It provides instant access to information about courses, events, admissions, and facilities.

Our Mission

We aim to empower students with quick access to university information, reducing repetitive queries and helping informed decisions.

How It Works

SmartBot uses AI and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand queries and provide accurate answers instantly, 24/7.

Key Features

Course & Admission Info

Quick guidance for available courses and admission procedures.

Campus Events

Stay updated on workshops, events, and campus activities.

FAQ Assistance

Instant answers to frequently asked questions anytime.

Campus Resources

Explore facilities, libraries, and labs efficiently.
