About PUGC SmartBot

PUGC SmartBot is an AI-powered virtual assistant designed to simplify the campus experience for students, staff, and visitors of Punjab University Gujranwala Campus. It provides instant access to information about courses, events, admissions, and campus facilities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with quick and reliable access to university information, reducing the need for repetitive queries and helping them make informed decisions about their academic and campus life.

How It Works

The SmartBot leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand your questions and provide accurate answers. Users can ask about course details, campus events, admissions, and more, receiving instant responses 24/7.

Key Features

• Access detailed course information and admission guidance.
• Stay updated on campus events and workshops.
• Get answers to frequently asked questions instantly.
• Explore campus facilities and resources efficiently.
